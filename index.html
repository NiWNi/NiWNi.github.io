About

I am an architect, one of those from the old school who builds buildings, but recently I have discovered my passion designing and building digital worlds.

All came from designing scripts in Dynamo to extend the potential of Revit and to facilitate architects to transition from CAD to BIM keeping some essential steps from the all workflow of the office to BIM.

I create websites form scratch, from design to development, easy to mantain and modify with clear handwritten code.

Besides that, a huge fan of everything that is genuinely good, from Wrigth´s masterpieces to literature´s genius, interested in behavioral theories, new tech and photography.

Thanks for reading until here, would be wonderful if we can collaborate in some amazing projects.
