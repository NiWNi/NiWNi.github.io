About

Formed as architect, I am an art lover and passionate geek who is delighted to combine design with my coding and web development skills to build amazing and beautiful apps and websites oriented to improve user experience.

My latest passion, coding, was discovered while I was designing scripts in Dynamo to extend the potential of Revit and to provide a smooth transition from CAD to BIM to a team of architects. While it was easy to achieve a great amount of improvements compared to the standard OTB software, I was craving for more and started to learn Python and dig into the API. Few months after, Google launched Google challenge with Udacity and thanks to them, here I am, doing two things I am truly passionate about: programming and design.

Besides that, I am a huge fan of everything that is genuinely well made, from F.L. Wrigth´s breathtaking works to Nabokov or García Márquez heavyweight novels. I enjoy exploring new technologies, read about behavioural theories and every now and then, shoot some photographies.

Take a further look into my CV or Portfolio or just share your thoughts in the contact section. Thanks for taking the time to read me.
